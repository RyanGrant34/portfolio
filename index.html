The Default Nudge
"40% of Canadian millennials have under $500 saved."
Designed a behavioral economics intervention using default-choice architecture: auto-transfer 5% of payroll to a locked savings account, opt-out only. 600 simulated participants, P<.005 significance, 2-year quasi-experimental design. Applied Thaler & Sunstein's nudge theory to a real financial services context to drive measurable savings behaviour change.

Entrepreneurship

O'Nattys Apparel.

At 19, I built O'Nattys Apparel from nothing into a streetwear brand placed into boutiques across Ontario. No co-founder. No funding. Just a product idea, a Shopify store, and the willingness to cold-call boutiques and market operators until they said yes.

I designed the hats in Photoshop, managed overseas manufacturing relationships, handled customs and logistics, built a wholesale pricing model, and ran two complete seasonal collections, the SS 2023 White Collection and SS 2024 Black Collection. The brand had a distinctive identity and a real customer base.

Two years of operation compressed years of real business education: sales, ops, supply chain, brand, and capital management all at once.
